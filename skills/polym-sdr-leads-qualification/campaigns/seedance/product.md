## Product: Seedance

Seedance is ByteDance's AI video generation platform — the most advanced AI video model across all parameters. It produces usable video approximately 90% of the time on the very first generation attempt, due to strong prompt adherence.

Key strengths:
- Industry-leading prompt adherence and first-try success rate
- Seedance Omni feature: combines multiple references (images, video, audio) in a single generation
- Physical dynamics, motion fidelity, cinematic movement, complex environments
- 4K output capability
- Direct API access with advanced capabilities for enterprise integrations
- Designed for high-volume creative production workflows at scale

Primary use cases by segment:
- Film studios: post-production automation, B-roll generation, VFX support
- Broadcast networks: promo generation, trailers, news graphics, episodic support
- Short drama producers: high-volume vertical video generation
- AI tools companies: API integration, embedded video generation pipeline
- Creative agencies: ad variant generation, faster client delivery
- Content creators: daily content at scale, posting velocity
- Advertising teams: video ad creatives, A/B test variants, localization
