## Kling AI ICP — Real Target Verticals (Priority Order)
1. Premium film studios and narrative production companies focused on cinematic quality output
2. Fashion, luxury, and beauty brands running high-end video campaigns
3. Commercial production companies producing TV commercials and brand films
4. VFX studios and post-production houses needing realistic scene generation or character work
5. Gaming companies and interactive entertainment studios needing character animation or cinematic content
6. AI tools companies building realism-first video generation products (API integrations)
7. Advertising agencies working on premium or luxury brand accounts
8. Photo studios and photography agencies extending stills into video
9. High-end content creators and influencer studios focused on production quality (adjacent)

## Target Personas (Priority Order)
- Founder, CEO, Managing Director — full budget authority
- Creative Director, Head of Production, VFX Supervisor, Director of Photography — cinematic quality owners and workflow decision makers
- Executive Producer, Head of Content, VP of Creative — budget owners and creative leads
- CTO / CPO / AI Lead — if building AI products or pipelines with video generation
- Brand Director, Head of Brand, Marketing Director — for fashion/luxury/beauty buyer segment
- Senior Producer, Lead Animator, Senior VFX Artist, Senior Motion Designer — strong champions and technical influencers

NOT A TARGET (score buyer_fit 0–5): Junior roles, coordinators, interns, assistants, analysts.
Also: Creative Partner, Creative AI Partner — partnership/vendor-facing roles with no production budget authority.

JUNIOR ROLE = HARD DISQUALIFY: intern, junior, coordinator, assistant, analyst without seniority.
