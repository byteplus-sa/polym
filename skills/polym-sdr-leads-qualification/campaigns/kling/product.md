## Product: Kling AI

Kling AI is Kuaishou's cinematic AI video generation platform, purpose-built for buyers who prioritize visual realism, character fidelity, and cinematic production quality over speed or volume.

Key strengths:
- Best-in-class realistic motion synthesis — natural physics, fluid movement, lifelike character animation
- Strong character consistency across frames — same face, costume, and expression throughout a clip
- Long-form video generation (up to 3 minutes per clip), enabling narrative storytelling
- Industry-leading image-to-video: photorealistic animation from a single still image
- Cinematic camera controls: dolly, pan, tilt, zoom, tracking shots
- Enterprise API access for integration into production pipelines
- Strong performance on fashion, luxury, beauty, and character-driven narratives

Primary use cases by segment:
- Premium film and narrative studios: cinematic scene generation, character-driven sequences
- Fashion and luxury brands: product showcase videos, model animation from still photography
- Commercial production companies: high-end brand films, TV commercial prototyping
- VFX and post-production houses: realistic scene extension, plate replacement, motion reference
- Gaming and entertainment: character animation, cinematic cutscenes, in-game video assets
- Advertising agencies (premium end): luxury brand campaigns, high-fidelity creative prototypes
- AI tools companies: API integration for realism-first video generation pipelines
