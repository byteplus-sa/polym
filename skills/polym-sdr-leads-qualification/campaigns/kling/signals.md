## Kling Signal Interpretation

Base signal hierarchy (POST → COMMENT → LIKE), company momentum signals, and the new executive signal are defined in common/signal-taxonomy.md.

## Kling-Specific Signal Notes

A like from a Creative Director, VFX Supervisor, or Brand Director on Kling content is medium-low — NOT low.
A like on Kling-specific content (realism demos, character fidelity examples, image-to-video showcases, cinematic quality comparisons) from a strong-fit company/persona is stronger than a generic like.

## Kling-Specific Buying Signals

High-intent:
- Posts or comments about character consistency, visual realism, or cinematic quality in AI video
- Comparing AI video tools for a premium production project
- Exploring image-to-video for fashion, luxury, or beauty content
- VFX supervisor or Director of Photography asking about AI tools for scene generation

Medium-intent:
- Following or commenting on AI video generation demos (realism, character work)
- Posts about AI adoption in a production workflow or VFX pipeline
- Hiring for AI-integrated creative or VFX roles
- Attending or presenting at digital production or VFX conferences

Company momentum signals specific to Kling (flag in observed_evidence):
- Fashion or luxury brand expanding into video content
- Production company adding an AI/technology department
- New Creative Director or Head of Production recently hired (< 12 months)
- Recent brand campaign requiring high-volume high-quality video output
